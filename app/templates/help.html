🆘 Help & FAQ

Answers to common questions about using ClassForge effectively.

Navigate to the Student Allocator page, upload your CSV file, set the desired number of classes, adjust the sliders, and click Allocate Students.

The Academic Weight slider controls the importance of academic performance. The Well-being Weight controls the social distribution. Together they must always total 100%.

After allocation, two graphs will be displayed: one using ClassForge AI and one using random distribution. You can also compare by class using the dropdown menu.
